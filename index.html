What is the minimum age required to rent a scooter?

To rent a scooter bike with us, you must be at least 18 years old and have a valid driver 's license. We take your safety seriously, and that 's why we have this policy in place. This requirement is in line with the Greek government 's laws and regulations.

What documents do I need to rent a scooter motorbike?

For renting a scooter or motorbike in Greece, you will need a valid driver 's license that allows you to operate a motorcycle. If you are a citizen of the EU, you can use your national driver 's license. For non-EU citizens, you need an International Driving Permit (IDP) in addition to your national driver 's license.

It 's important to note that your driver 's license should have a category AM, A1, A2 or A, which permits you to operate a motorcycle. Finally, it is recommended to bring a copy of your passport, just in case it is needed for identification purposes.

Is a deposit required before renting scooter motorbike?

For our scooter motorbike rentals in Philippines, we do not require a deposit from our customers. You simply need to pay for the rental cost at the time of pick up.

Do you provide insurance coverage?

Yes, we provide insurance coverage for all our rental scooter motorbikes and it is included in the final price. Our insurance covers you for third-party liability and damage to the vehicle. However, there is an excess amount that you will be responsible for in case of an accident or damage to the vehicle. The excess amount varies depending on the type of motorbike you rent, and you will be informed about the excess amount before you sign the rental agreement.

Please note that our insurance coverage is void if the driver is under the influence of drugs or alcohol, driving recklessly, or not following traffic rules and regulations. In case of an accident, it is important to inform us immediately and file a police report. If the damage is significant, we will provide you with a replacement motorbike while the damaged one is being repaired.

If you have any further questions regarding our insurance coverage, please feel free to contact us.

Are there any hidden charges or fees?

No, there are no hidden charges or fees when you rent a scooter motorbike from us. The price you see on our website or receive from our staff includes everything - the bike rental cost, all accessories and extras, and insurance coverage.

You can rest assured that there will be no surprises or additional charges when you return the scooter motorbike. We believe in providing transparent and fair pricing to our customers, so you can enjoy your rental experience without any worries.

Do you have helmets available?

Yes, at no additional cost. We take the safety of our customers seriously and provide helmets for both the driver and passenger of the scooter. We understand that helmets are a crucial part of safe driving and we make sure that all our helmets are new, clean, and in excellent condition.

We also have a strict disinfection process that we follow for each helmet immediately after a rental has ended. This ensures that you will get a helmet in mint condition, clean and fresh. We want you to have a pleasant and safe experience while riding our scooters, and providing you with high-quality helmets is just one of the ways we ensure your safety.

How many miles are included per day of rental?

At Motor Rental Philippines, we offer unlimited mileage for each day of rental. We believe that you should be able to enjoy your ride around Philippines without worrying about any mileage limits. Therefore, you don 't need to keep track of your distance or pay extra fees for additional miles. Just relax and explore the beautiful islands at your own pace. Our goal is to provide you with a hassle-free rental experience and make your stay in Greece unforgettable.
